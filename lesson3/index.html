Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to bring up to date information and content to children and youth. There are plenty of weather sites out there for adults. However, the amount of kid friendly sites is fairly small. This site will provide 5 day weather forcast, up to date current weather, a weather gallery, and a storm reporting page.
