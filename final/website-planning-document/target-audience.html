Target Auidence

My target auidence is children from the ages of 6-14. Our target demographic is native english speaking people. We are hope to provide better content for children and youth to access weather and fun information.

Persona 1

Elend Venture

Elend is an 8-year-old from Preston Idaho. He is is obsessed with the weather and is always checking the forcast. Elend likes to attend activities in the park and other local events with his friends.

Persona 2

Valette Renoux

Vallette is a french 6-year-old girl who is moving to Fish Haven Idaho for the summer. She is learning about the weather and loves seeing photos of storms. She loves to watch for storms and is looking for a kid friendly place to report storm sitings.

Scenerio 1

Yes! On our site we have a section deadicated to local events coming up! Special events will be displayed on the top of the page a day before. So be sure to check back often!

Scenerio 2

Of course! Vallette will find an easy to use form that will allow her to fill out all the information and even attach images from the storm!

Scenerio 3

Elend will be happy to see that our weather site offers a 5 day weather forcast! Also displayed is the current weather updated hourly!

Scenerio 4

Vallette will want to browse our storm gallery page. There she will find many pictures of storms. She will be able to filter by the type of storm she is looking for!

Scenerio 5

Our site offers weather for Fish Haven, Soda Springs, and Preston Idaho! We are currently working to add the ability to search the weather in other locations!
